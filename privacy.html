Privacy Statement

This statement explains what information Stag Head Limited collects when you use this extension, how it is used, where it is stored, and what your rights are under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

Controller Stag Head Limited
Last updated 24 April 2026
Applicable law NZ Privacy Act 2020
§ 01

Who We Are

Stag Head Limited (Companies Register No. 6750773, NZBN 9429046638891) is the publisher of this extension for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central. We are a New Zealand limited company and are the agency responsible for personal information collected in connection with this extension.

References to "we", "us", or "Stag Head" in this statement mean Stag Head Limited. References to "you" or "your" mean the organisation or individual using the extension.

§ 02

What the Extension Does

The extension connects your Microsoft Business Central environment to one or more third-party platforms. It enables data to flow between Business Central and those platforms to support the extension's functionality.

Stag Head operates a small set of Azure cloud services to act as a bridge between Business Central and any connected third-party platforms. These services handle authorisation flows and route status updates back to your Business Central company. They do not store your business data.

§ 03

What Information We Collect and Why

Stag Head collects only the minimum technical routing information needed to operate the bridge services. We do not collect or store your business records or any personal financial information — that data remains exclusively within your own Business Central environment.

Data Why we hold it Where it is stored
OAuth2 access token Returned to us temporarily during the authorisation flow. We write it into your Business Central setup record immediately and retain a mapping so we can identify which BC tenant it belongs to. Azure Table Storage (Australia East)
Your Business Central tenant ID, environment name, and company ID Used to route the access token and status updates to the correct Business Central company. Not linked to any individual user — these are organisational identifiers. Azure Table Storage (Australia East)
Payment identifiers Stored alongside your BC company identifiers so that webhook notifications can be routed to the correct payment record in Business Central. Azure Table Storage (Australia East)

We do not use this information for any purpose other than operating the extension. We do not use it for marketing, profiling, or analytics.

§ 04

Information Held in Your Business Central Environment

The extension stores additional data directly inside your Business Central environment. This data is held under your organisation's own Microsoft tenancy and is subject to Microsoft's terms and privacy policies, not Stag Head's. We do not have access to this data except when acting on your instructions through the Business Central API during normal operation of the extension.

This includes:

  • OAuth2 tokens (stored in the extension setup table);
  • A cache of any connected account data retrieved from third-party platforms;
  • Imported records and transaction data;
  • Payment records and their status history.

You can delete any of this data at any time from within Business Central. Uninstalling the extension from Business Central will remove all extension tables and their data from your environment.

§ 05

Who We Share Information With

We share information only as necessary to operate the extension:

  • Third-party platform providers — We pass your authorisation request to any connected platform's OAuth2 endpoints to establish your connection. Each platform's use of your data is governed by their own privacy notice.
  • Microsoft Corporation — The extension operates within Business Central (Microsoft's platform) and uses Azure (Microsoft's cloud infrastructure) for our bridge services. Microsoft's use of data in this context is governed by the Microsoft Trust Centre and your organisation's Microsoft agreement.

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any other third parties.

§ 06

Where Your Information is Stored

The routing data described in § 03 is stored in Microsoft Azure Table Storage hosted in the Australia East region (located in New South Wales, Australia).

Australia has been assessed by the New Zealand Privacy Commissioner as providing comparable privacy safeguards to New Zealand under the Privacy Act 2020. The transfer of data to Australia East is therefore permitted without additional safeguards under Information Privacy Principle 12.

§ 07

How Long We Keep Your Information

We retain routing data only for as long as your Subscription is active. When your Subscription ends — whether by cancellation, non-renewal, or termination — we will delete all routing data associated with your Business Central tenant as promptly as practicable.

You may also request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at help@staghead.nz. We will action deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe, typically no more than 30 days.

Payment mapping records (payment ID → BC entry) are retained for the duration of your Subscription to support ongoing webhook routing and will be deleted on termination alongside all other routing data.

§ 08

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the routing data we hold against loss, unauthorised access, use, modification, or disclosure. These steps include:

  • Storing data in Azure Table Storage with access restricted by Azure Key Vault-managed connection strings;
  • Using HTTPS for all communications between the extension, our bridge services, third-party platforms, and Business Central;
  • Validating webhook payloads using HMAC-SHA256 signature verification to prevent spoofed requests;
  • Using function-level authentication keys for internal API calls between our Azure Functions and Business Central.

No system is completely secure. If you become aware of a security issue related to the extension, please notify us immediately at help@staghead.nz.

§ 09

Your Rights Under the Privacy Act 2020

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of any personal information we hold about you;
  • Correction — request correction of any personal information that is inaccurate or out of date;
  • Deletion — request deletion of your data (subject to any legal obligations to retain it);
  • Complaint — make a complaint to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner at privacy.org.nz if you believe we have interfered with your privacy.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at help@staghead.nz. We will respond within the timeframes required by the Privacy Act.

Note: most of the information processed by the extension relates to organisations (Business Central tenant identifiers), not to identified individuals. Where personal information is involved — such as if your name appears in a payment description — we will treat access and correction requests accordingly.

§ 10

Third-Party Services and Your Consent

Third-party platform providers connected to this extension act as independent controllers of any data held within their systems. When you authorise the extension to connect to a third-party platform, you are entering into a separate relationship with that provider governed by their own terms and privacy notice.

Stag Head does not have access to your credentials for any third-party platform at any point. Each platform manages your connections directly and provides data to the extension using a scoped access token that you authorise through the platform's own authorisation flow.

You can review and revoke the extension's access to any connected platform at any time through that platform's account management tools. Revoking access will prevent future data imports or submissions but will not affect data already imported into Business Central.

We encourage you to review this statement periodically. Continued use of the extension after changes are published constitutes your acceptance of the updated statement.
