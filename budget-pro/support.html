Response time: We aim to respond to all support enquiries within 2 business days (New Zealand time). For urgent issues affecting your ability to use the extension, please note "URGENT" in your subject line.

Before contacting support

Please include the following information in your support request to help us assist you faster:

  • Your Business Central version (Settings → Help & Support → Troubleshooting)
  • A description of what you were trying to do and what happened instead
  • Any error messages you saw (a screenshot is helpful)
  • The name or code of the Budget Assignment or Budget Cycle involved, if applicable
